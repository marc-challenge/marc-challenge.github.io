# Notices &amp; Policies

These three notices apply across all channels and are also summarized in the site footer.

## 1 · Offline judging runtime

The competition **runtime has no external network access**. Calls to public APIs
are **not allowed** during judging.

> **Build time *is* online.** Bake model weights and dependencies into your image
> at build time (internet available then). At runtime, **no downloads and no
> external API calls**.

## 2 · Third-party assets — license &amp; attribution

MARC uses third-party assets, including **open-source software (OSS)** and **USD**
content. Their licenses and sources are acknowledged here, and this list reflects
the outcome of USD license review.

- A consolidated list of OSS / USD licenses and attributions is provided with the
  starter kit and developer guide.
- Participants must respect the licenses of all bundled and downloaded assets.

## 3 · The competition background (playground) may change

The only published background USD is the **practice `chungmu`** environment. The
**actual competition may run on a different background** — this prevents overfitting
to, or exposure of, the final stage.

## Tone &amp; terminology

- Public materials are written in a **consultative tone** — "under internal review"
  and "(proposed)" rather than "finalized" — pending co-chair agreement.
- Stage 2's destination is referred to as the **"designated location."**

## Non-disclosure boundary

Answer coordinates, scoring constants, internal seeds, operational backends, and
ground-truth data are **not disclosed**. Scoring is described at the level of
**methodology only**.
