## How archives work

- Each finished edition is **frozen** at a fixed path (`/{year}/`) as of that year's
  close and is not edited afterward.
- The root (`/`) always shows the **current** edition.
- The index at **`/editions/`** links to every snapshot.
- Archived editions pin their external links (starter kit, guide) to that edition's
  version (`v{year}.x`) so they don't break.
